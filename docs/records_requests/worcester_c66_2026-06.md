## Request

Under the Massachusetts Public Records Law, **M.G.L. c. 66, § 10**, and the definition of
"public records" in **M.G.L. c. 4, § 7, cl. 26**, I request copies of the following records
held by the City of Worcester.

I request the **complete, integrated base collective bargaining agreements (CBAs)** — i.e., the
full underlying contract documents containing all standard articles (including but not limited
to interest arbitration, grievance/arbitration, wage comparability, and no-strike provisions),
together with all appendices, wage/salary schedules, and side letters — for the following three
bargaining units, for any agreement **in effect at any point during January 1, 2014 through
December 31, 2024**:

1. **Worcester Fire Fighters, Local 1009, I.A.F.F.** (uniformed fire)
2. **Local 490, NAGE/SEIU** (clerical / former Worcester Clerical Association)
3. **Local 170, Teamsters** (DPW clerks)

### Clarifying scope (important)

The City's Human Resources portal currently posts **successor "Memoranda of Agreement" (MOAs)**
for these units (e.g., the July 1, 2017 – June 30, 2020 MOAs). Those MOAs amend wage and benefit
terms but state that they "supplement or supersede inconsistent terms of prior agreements" and
are pending "the drafting of a new contract document." **This request is for the full base
contract documents that those MOAs incorporate by reference — not the MOAs alone.**

Specifically, for each unit I am requesting:
- the most recent **fully restated / consolidated base CBA** that the 2014–2024 MOAs amend; and
- any **predecessor or successor full base CBA** whose term overlaps the 2014–2024 window; and
- any executed **final contract document** produced after a MOA (if the "new contract document"
  referenced in a MOA was subsequently drafted and executed).

If a single consolidated base agreement does not exist for a unit and the operative contract is
instead an older base CBA as amended by a series of MOAs, please provide that base CBA plus the
full set of amending MOAs for the window, so the complete operative text can be reconstructed.

response **within 10 business days** of receipt. If the request cannot be fulfilled in that
