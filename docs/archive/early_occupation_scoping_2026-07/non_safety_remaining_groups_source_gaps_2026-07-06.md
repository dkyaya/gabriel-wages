## Cross-cutting note on Massachusetts vs. national evidence

For all three groups, this session's Massachusetts-specific evidence is thinner than national context, mirroring the pattern already flagged for sanitation and transit: national nursing-shortage and dispatcher-reclassification data are well-sourced and current, but city-specific Massachusetts confirmation (beyond the two already-collected custodial rows and the Arlington/Wayland bundled-unit fragments) remains largely unexplored. Future work should keep national-general and Massachusetts-specific/project-specific evidence explicitly distinguished, per the discipline already established across every prior occupation-group memo in this project.
